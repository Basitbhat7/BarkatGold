Barkat Group for Foodstuff and Consumer Products. was established in 2004 with modest beginnings and over the years emerged as the prime importer, retailer, processor and distributor of authentic, premium high quality Spices, pulses, herbs and other foodstuff in Kashmir.

We are and certified company with stringent standards for high quality products, state of the art processing methods, professional management, ethical business practices. We keep our customer’s needs foremost and this has propelled us from a modest beginnings, with a planned vision, to our current standing as one of Kashmir’s prime foodstuff supplier, and with prospects for a more enhanced future growth.

Our Corporate identity has been built on the strength of our robust business understanding of our customer’s needs and we are happy to be recognized for leaving a positive imprint on all our business relationships and also in reinforcing bonds and trust with our valued customers which transcends business.
